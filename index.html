hill stations

wilson hills

Wilson Hills is a hill station in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is near Dharampur Taluka and is only on Valsad District. It is a beautiful place

doodhpatri

Doodhpathri is a tourist destination and a hill station located in the Khansahib tehsil of the Budgam district of Jammu and Kashmir, India.

ponmudi

Ponmudi is a popular tourist destination in Kerala and offers visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and enjoy the tranquillity

shimla

Situated in the Himalayas, Shimla is one of the most well known hill stations in India. It is a token of India’s colonial history along with its astonishing natural beauty.

manali

Another one of India’s most visited hill station, Manali sits in the Beas River Valley, giving its tourists the most breathtaking views.

leh-ladakh

A little more up north sits Leh-Ladakh in all its glory. With its irresistible landscapes and vibrant yet deep cultural heritage, the beauty of this place is like a dream.

ooty

When talking about hill stations, South Indian locations usually don’t come to mind. Once you know more about Ooty, you start planning your next vacation.

munnar

A small town nestled in Kerala’s Western Ghat mountain range, Munnar is another one of the most famous South Indian hill stations.
